Espacenet may reject your requests if you are using any automated tools, perform too many queries per minute or generate queries that result in the system attempting to retrieve unusually large numbers of documents or unusually large documents. Please note that multiple users behind a firewall may be seen as a single user who apparently generates many queries in a short time or who downloads excessive amounts of data. As a result it is possible that a colleague other than the user who triggered the error will receive a rejection message. If you feel the rejection is not warranted and you would like us to investigate it, please contact us and send us your IP address, which can be found by searching for "what is my IP" on the net (please note it is the public IP we need so it cannot start with 10, 192.168 or 172.16), your timezone and the time the rejection message was received: document.write((new Date()).toDateString()) at document.write((new Date()).toLocaleTimeString()) We will analyse the information you send us and if necessary contact your IT department to try to resolve the situation. For more information please click on the "terms and conditions of use for Espacenet" link at the bottom of the home page.
