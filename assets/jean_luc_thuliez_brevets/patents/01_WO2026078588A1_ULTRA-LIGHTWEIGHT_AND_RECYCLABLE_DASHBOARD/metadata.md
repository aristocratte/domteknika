## Abrégé

The invention relates to a dashboard (01) comprising at least one main part (02, 03, 04) made of PPE, said dashboard (01) being recyclable, ultra-lightweight, acoustically and thermally insulating, easy to assemble and disassemble, designed to operate with thermal expansions, said dashboard including crash protection elements and integrating numerous features directly molded into the shape of the parts, in particular air circulation circuits.
