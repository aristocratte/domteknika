## Abrégé

Forming a perform from at least two materials by using a single injector to introduce them successively into a mould so the first material contacts the mould surface, then the second material contacts the first material and then the injector is closed. The injector has separate conduits for each material and a member sliding in a central bore. The member has successive positions for admitting the first material, second material and closing the injector. Independent claims are included for the following: (a) Apparatus for performing the above method. (b) A perform produced by the above method. Preferred Features: The front end of the member is concave and, when the injector is closed, it shapes the end of the perform. The amount of both materials supplied by the injector compensates for their cooling during the injection process.
