## Abrégé

[0001] The invention relates to an air intake device for an internal combustion engine, in particular for electronic injection engines. Said device comprises a housing ( 11) and a mobile air flow regulating member ( 12) housed in an inner cavity ( 13) of said housing. The mobile member comprises a central passage ( 16) providing communication between an inlet ( 14 a) and an outlet ( 14 b) and circulation of an air flow. The adjustable member (12) rotates about an axis of rotation (15) and can allow all the positions between the open and closed positions. The inner cavity (13) is delimited by at least one surface of the coating (20), and the regulating member (12) comprises at least one surface of the coating (30) designed to co-operate with the surface of the coating (20) so as to be pressed against each other in the closed position. The center of curvature (17) of the surface of revolution (30) of the regulating member (12) is offset relative to the axis of rotation (15) of its regulating member.[0001] The invention relates to an air intake device for a heat engine, in particular for electronic injection engines. This device comprises a housing (11) and a movable member (12) for regulating the air flow housed in an inner cavity (13) of this housing. The movable member comprises a central passage ( 16) to ensure communication between an inlet opening ( 14 a) and an outlet opening ( 14 b) and to allow the circulation of an air flow. The movable member ( 12) for regulating the air flow is rotatable about an axis of rotation ( 15) and can adopt all the positions between two extreme positions called open and closed positions. The inner cavity is delimited by at least one first surface of revolution ( 20) and the movable member for regulating the air flow comprises at least one second surface of revolution ( 30) which bear against one another in the closed position. The device is characterized in that the center of curvature (17) of said second surface of revolution (30) is offset relative to the axis of rotation (15).
