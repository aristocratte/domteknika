## Abrégé

The method involves performing treatment e.g. antireflective treatment, of watch glass (25) to reduce light reflected by a component of mechanical movement (11) of watch (10) and passage of the light through the watch glass, where the component is made of transparent material. The antireflective treatment is performed such that a spectral transmission curve of the component is granted to that of the watch glass for reducing a reflecting surface of the component of the watch.
